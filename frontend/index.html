📋 Fertilizer Quick Reference
Urea (46% N) — Cheapest N. Top dressing. Fast acting.
DAP (18-46-0) — N + P at sowing. Root development.
SSP (0-16-0+S) — Phosphorus + Sulfur. Cheaper than DAP.
MOP (0-0-60) — Potassium. Fruiting, disease resistance.
NPK 17:17:17 — Balanced. All growth stages.
FYM / Compost — 10-15 t/ha. Improves soil health.

💡 Water Saving Tips
💧 Drip saves 40–60% water vs flood irrigation
🌅 Irrigate early morning or evening to reduce evaporation
🌧️ Skip irrigation if 10+ mm rain forecast in 24 hours
📊 Sandy soil: every 3–4 days. Clay: every 6–8 days
🌱 Critical stages need most water: flowering & fruiting
